Section 1. Speech Examples of Different Tracing Methods for VC

This section gives speech examples applied to three Voice Conversion (VC) models, including AutoVC, SpeechSplit2.0, and YourTTS-VC. For each VC model, the speech examples are source speeches, target speeches, plain VC outputs (w/o tracing) and the traceable VC outputs produced by different baselines and our VoxTracer. These examples correspond to Section 4.2 in our main paper.
